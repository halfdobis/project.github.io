About Us

The purpose of this site is to create awareness for the young and/or upcoming software devs out there. It is also for the software devs in the making. You would find materials, tools and links to platforms which might be of use for enriching your knowledge in software development.

This site is originally created by Dobis Daniel. Dobis graduated from Les Cours Sonou Universite with a Bsc in Computer Science. She created this page to share the materials and tools she has ever worked with and more.

What We Do

We keep updating and uploading new materials, tools and platforms that comes to light so that users can have and make multiple choices according to what works for them. We try as much as possible to keep our users updated with tools.
